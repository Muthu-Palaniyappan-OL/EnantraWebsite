Enviropreneur

Register
29 Nov 22, 12:00 PM to 03 Dec 22, 12:05 PM IST
Enviropreneur is a team event to assess your creativity, marketing and designing skills with a slight twist to test your responsibility towards the environment. It appeals to the entrepreneur inside each of the participants by testing their problem-solving capabilities. Round Wise Breakup: Round 1: The participants will be given a product which is a threat to the environment and asked to come up with a solution to counter the issue in the form of an alternative product. 6 teams who have the most impressive and innovative solution will move on to the next round. Round 2: The final six teams will be asked to design a poster digitally on their phones or laptops, come up with a tagline and convince the judges to invest in their product.
